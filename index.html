What is the area of rectangle?
The area of a rectangle is the space that is present inside the boundries of a rectangle.
How to calculate area of rectangle?
To calculate area of rectangle you should use the following formula:
A = w * l
where:
l = length
w = width
Why use area of rectangle?
The area of rectangle is used in order to find the region enclosed by a rectangle.
